Resource Hub for Campus

CampusLink is a comprehensive and secure resource hub designed to streamline campus placements while ensuring enhanced privacy and data security. This platform serves as a centralized ecosystem where students can access placement opportunities, receive real-time updates from HR, and connect with industry professionals. It also features a secure resource-sharing system, allowing tutors to upload and distribute study materials while maintaining strict access controls. Additionally, an encrypted chat system enables seamless communication between students and faculty for doubt resolution.
